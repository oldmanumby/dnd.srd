### Traits

Flyby. The owl doesn’t provoke an Opportunity Attack when it flies out of an enemy’s reach.

### Actions

Talons. Melee Attack Roll: +4, reach 5 ft. Hit: 7 (1d10 + 2) Slashing damage.

Spellcasting. The owl casts one of the following spells, requiring no spell components and using Wisdom as the spellcasting ability: At Will: Detect Evil and Good, Detect Magic 1/Day: Clairvoyance
